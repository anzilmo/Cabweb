What is a Rental Car Security Deposit?

A rental car security deposit is a refundable amount held by the rental agency to cover potential damages, traffic violations, or additional charges during the rental period. The deposit is typically blocked on your credit or debit card and released upon returning the vehicle in good condition. The exact amount may vary depending on the car type, rental agency, and location.

Can I Cancel or Modify My Reservation?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation, depending on the rental agency’s policies. Many agencies offer flexible cancellation or modification options, but there may be fees or time restrictions. We recommend checking the terms and conditions or contacting us directly to ensure a smooth process if you need to make any changes to your booking.

Is it Possible to Extend My Rental Period?

Additional charges may apply, depending on the extension duration and vehicle availability.
